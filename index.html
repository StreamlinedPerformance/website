About us

Founded during the 2020 Lockdown, Streamlined Performance is devoted to developing long-term change in swimmers around New Zealand. It’s our mission to provide coaches and swimmers with ongoing support to help create real change and lasting results. In the past swimming has been about swimming lengths until you’re sick of it; we make performance practical, skilful and fun.
